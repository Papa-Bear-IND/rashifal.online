Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters. This page explains exactly what information Rashifal.online collects, how it is used, and the rights you have over your data.

1. Information We Collect

Rashifal.online is designed to be a low-friction, read-only experience. We do not require accounts, logins, or any personal information to read your daily rashifal, check compatibility, or browse our tools.

The only data we collect is standard website analytics — anonymised information about how visitors use the site. This includes:

  • Pages viewed and time spent on the site
  • Approximate geographic region (city/country level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referring website (where you arrived from)

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. Email Capture for Notifications

If you voluntarily submit your email through our "Notify me" form for upcoming tools (Free Kundli, Janampatri, Panchang), we store it solely to inform you when those tools launch. We will never share, sell, or use your email for unrelated marketing.

3. Cookies

We use cookies for two purposes:

  • Analytics — to understand site usage patterns and improve content.
  • Advertising — Google AdSense may serve personalised ads using cookies. You can opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Your browser settings allow you to block or delete cookies at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on a small number of third parties to operate the site:

  • Google Fonts — for typography
  • Google AdSense — for advertising
  • Analytics providers — for visitor metrics

Each of these has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data.

5. Compliance with DPDP Act 2023

Rashifal.online complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 of India. Under this Act, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or erasure of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for any data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect any data we hold. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
